Pizza

No pizza oven? No problem! Make your homemade pizza on the grill for that classic smoky, wood fired oven flavor. And even if you have a pizza oven, you might prefer the grill from time to time.

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ cup tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes, divided
  • ¼ cup roasted red peppers, drained and chopped, divided
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until the cheese melts
